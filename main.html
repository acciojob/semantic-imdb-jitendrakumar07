Summary

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. Sentenced to life imprisonment at Shawshank Penitentiary, he befriends Red, a fellow inmate who helps him navigate the brutal realities of prison life. Despite the corruption and despair that surround him, Andy clings to hope and a dream of freedom, eventually orchestrating an audacious escape that offers redemption not only for himself but also for those he leaves behind.
